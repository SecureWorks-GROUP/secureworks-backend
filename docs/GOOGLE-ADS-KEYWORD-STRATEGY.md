### Match Type Strategy
- **Exact match** for high-intent core terms (protect budget)
- **Phrase match** for long-tail and question keywords (capture variations)
- **NEVER use broad match** unless paired with Smart Bidding and significant conversion data (100+ conversions/month)

### Bidding Strategy
- Start with **Manual CPC** or **Enhanced CPC** for the first 2-4 weeks to gather data
- Move to **Target CPA** once you have 30+ conversions
- Set Target CPA at $60-$80 for patios, $50-$70 for fencing, $50-$70 for decking
- Use **bid adjustments**: +20% for mobile, +15% for 7am-7pm, +10% for high-income suburbs

### Geographic Targeting
- Target Perth metro + surrounds (approximately 50km radius from Perth CBD)
- Extend to Mandurah in the south, Yanchep/Two Rocks in the north
- Exclude rural WA (too far to service profitably)
- Use radius targeting, NOT "people interested in" -- avoid showing ads to people outside your service area

## 8. Landing Page Strategy

### Key Principles

1. **One landing page per service category** -- do not send all traffic to the homepage
   - `/patios/` -- for patio keywords
   - `/insulated-patios/` -- for insulated patio keywords (your differentiator)
   - `/fencing/` -- for fencing keywords
   - `/decking/` -- for decking keywords

2. **Message match** -- the headline on the landing page MUST echo the ad headline the user clicked

3. **Above the fold must include:**
   - Clear headline matching the search intent
   - Suburb/area mention (via dynamic text replacement)
   - Hero image of completed project
   - Single, prominent CTA (phone number + form)
   - Trust signals (years experience, number of projects, Google rating)

4. **Page structure:**
   - Hero with CTA
   - 3-4 key benefits with icons
   - Photo gallery / before-after
   - Social proof (Google reviews, testimonials)
   - Pricing guide / cost estimator ($250-$650/m2 range)
   - FAQ section (targets "people also ask" keywords)
   - Final CTA with form

5. **Technical requirements:**
   - Load time under 3 seconds (affects Quality Score)
   - Mobile-first design (60%+ of traffic is mobile)
   - Click-to-call button prominently placed
   - Form above the fold (name, phone, email, suburb, service interest)
   - Thank you page with conversion tracking pixel

6. **Competitor landing page weaknesses to exploit:**
   - Most competitors send traffic to their homepage (bad practice)
   - Few have cost/pricing information on landing pages
   - Social proof is often generic, not project-specific
   - Load times are poor on most competitor sites
   - Mobile experience is typically an afterthought

## Important Caveats

1. **Search volume estimates are approximations.** Exact Perth-specific search volumes require Google Keyword Planner access with an active Google Ads account. The figures provided are triangulated from multiple industry sources, relative search trends, and Australian market data. They serve as directional guidance for prioritization.

2. **CPC ranges fluctuate.** CPCs change based on competition, seasonality (Perth patio season peaks September-March), Quality Score, and bid strategy. The ranges provided represent typical bands observed in the Australian home services market.

3. **These estimates are conservative for Perth.** Perth generally has 10-20% lower CPCs than Sydney/Melbourne due to lower advertiser competition, which is a significant advantage.

4. **Validate with live data.** Once campaigns are running, the Search Terms Report will provide the actual keywords triggering ads and their real CPCs. This document should be treated as a starting framework to be refined with live performance data.

5. **Seasonality matters.** Perth outdoor living searches peak from September through March (spring/summer). Budget should be weighted 60-70% toward these months.
