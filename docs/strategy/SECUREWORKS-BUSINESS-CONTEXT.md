## 4. WHAT WE DO — ALL DIVISIONS

### Insulated Patios (Primary Revenue Driver — Highest Margin)
Insulated panel roof systems (SolarSpan by Bondor, Stratco Outback/Pavilion/Ambient, Metroll Alto) that create genuine extensions of the home. Engineered for Perth's climate.
- Roof styles: Flat/Skillion, Gable, Flyover, Freestanding
- Panel options: 50mm (R1.2), 75mm (R1.8), 100mm (R2.4)
- Ceiling finishes: Plain (modern), VJ Lined (traditional)
- Spans 8+ metres without posts, service channels for electrical
- **Price range:** $12,000 – $60,000+
- **2025 target:** $3.0M revenue

### Colorbond Fencing (Cash Engine — Most Systemised)
Perth's boundary standard. Heights: 1.5m, 1.8m, 2.1m. Solid panels, slat fencing, gates (swing/sliding), pool fencing, retaining with plinths.
- **Price range:** $3,000 – $15,000 (~$115/metre)
- **2025 target:** $1.5M revenue

### Emergency Services / Makesafes (Winter Hedge — Ramping Up)
Insurance and B2B focused division that provides counter-seasonal revenue when outdoor living work slows in winter.
- Makesafes (storm damage, fallen trees, structural emergencies)
- Temporary repairs
- Minor restoration
- Emergency call-outs
- Asbestos fencing removal (Category B)
- B2B partnerships with insurance builders and loss adjusters
- **2026 target:** $300K-$500K revenue (Year 1 of full commercial push)
- **Strategic importance:** Provides year-round cashflow stability. Outdoor services are seasonal (summer peak); emergency/insurance work fills the winter gap.

### Decking (Growth Division — Launching)
Composite (NewTechWood, Trex, ModWood, Ekodeck) and timber (Merbau, Spotted Gum, Jarrah, treated pine). Pool surrounds, elevated decks, ground-level platforms.
- **Price range:** $8,000 – $40,000+
- **Key differentiator:** Patio + deck integration — nobody else in Perth offers insulated roof + deck floor as one coordinated project

### Outdoor Living by SecureWorks Group (Premium Parent Brand)
The premium project brand capturing homeowners wanting $60-100K+ complete backyard transformations. Not a separate legal entity — a coordinated brand platform bringing multiple specialist operators together under one brand promise.
- Captures full-service clients wanting: patio + decking + fencing + astroturf + outdoor kitchen + lighting
- **Positioning statement:** "Perth's premium outdoor living specialists. We design, coordinate, and deliver complete backyard transformations — from concept to entertaining."
- **Brand promise:** "One call, one team, one result that lasts."

### Other Services (Cross-Sell / Add-On)

### Multi-Operator / Autonomous Division Model
Inspired by Cody Sanchez-style multi-operator model. Specialists run autonomous divisions. Each operator manages their own P&L for their trade (fencing, patios, decking, etc.). When an Outdoor Living project comes in, a dedicated project coordinator orchestrates execution across multiple trades.

- Deep expertise in each trade (no jack-of-all-trades generalists)

### Key Financial Observations
1. **Revenue is highly variable** — monthly combined revenue ranges from ~$70K to $160K+ depending on project timing
2. **Gross margin in SW WA is volatile** — COGS sometimes exceeds revenue in a given month (material purchases and labour hit before corresponding invoices are recognised)
3. **Fencing is the most consistent** revenue stream in SW WA ($25K-$74K/month)
4. **Patios is the biggest driver** — especially via Group entity ($71.5K in Oct alone)
5. **Makesafe revenue still small** — $224 to $6,284/month, but growing and proving the concept
6. **Reno/extension work** adds sporadic revenue ($0-$22.7K/month)
7. **Operating expenses in SW WA** run $20-40K/month (wages $9-20K, insurance ~$2K, advertising $1.6-3.9K, super $1.1-2.4K are biggest items)
8. **Net profit swings wildly** — from ($40K) loss to $30K+ profit month to month. Project timing and material cost timing are the main drivers.
