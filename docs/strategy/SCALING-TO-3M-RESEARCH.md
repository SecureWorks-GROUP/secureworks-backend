## 1. Executive Summary: The 5 Highest-Impact Moves

SecureWorks generates ~$1.4M/year from ~93 jobs. To reach $3M, you need ~200 jobs/year — roughly 9 more per month. Conversion (30%) and close cycle (1-2 weeks) are solid. **The bottleneck is lead volume.** Crews have capacity.

### The 5 Moves

1. **Set up customer financing (Handypay via HIA — $0 merchant fee)**
   - Adding "$89/week" to a $20K quote → +18% close rate, +30% avg job size
   - Costs nothing to set up. Amplifies every existing lead.
   - Expected: +$250-400K/year

2. **Launch structured referral program ($300-500 give/get)**
   - Australian solar co. case study: $8.46M from referrals in one year (38% conversion)
   - Zero referral system today. 93 past jobs as seed → even 15% referral rate = 14 extra jobs
   - Expected: +$200-350K/year

3. **Deploy AI phone answering (Johnni.ai — $150-300/month)**
   - Contractors miss 5-10 calls/week = $45-120K/year lost. 78% of callers who hit voicemail call next tradie.
   - Johnni.ai: Australian-built, trade jargon trained, 24/7. $150-300/mo vs $58K receptionist.
   - Expected: +$150-300K/year

4. **Scale Google Ads to $6-8K/month + add Facebook at $1-2K**
   - Perth CPCs 10-20% cheaper than Sydney/Melbourne
   - Add Facebook for 35-65 age bracket in premium suburbs
   - Expected: +$300-500K/year

5. **Build the Grand Slam Offer (Hormozi framework)**
   - Sell "an extra room that adds $30-50K property value" not "a patio"
   - Stack guarantees: price lock, timeline, clean site, quality inspection, satisfaction
   - Tiered pricing: 4+ options → 52% close rate vs 42%
   - Expected: +$140K/year from conversion lift

**Combined conservative: +$1.04M. Optimistic: +$1.69M. Total: $2.44-3.09M.**

## 2. Systemisation Blueprint

At $1.4M: CEO is 70% Technician / 20% Manager / 10% Entrepreneur.
At $3M: Must flip to 10% / 30% / 60%.

**The "Franchise Prototype" — 10 processes to document:**

### AI Changes the E-Myth Model

Where Gerber said "write a manual," AI in 2026 = dynamic digital guardrails embedded in tools (Secure Ops, Secure Trade). SOPs surface the right checklist at the right time. AI reduces onboarding time by 60%.

### EOS — Start With 3 Tools

1. **L10 Meetings** (weekly, 90 min): Scorecard review + Rock review + IDS (Identify/Discuss/Solve)
2. **Rocks** (90-day priorities): Hire 3rd crew leader. Document 10 SOPs. Launch maintenance subs. Hit $280K/month.
3. **Scorecard** (10 weekly KPIs): Leads, quotes, conversion, revenue, jobs, avg value, satisfaction, utilisation, AR days, warranty callbacks

### Hormozi Grand Slam Offer

**Value Equation:** (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)

**Current:** "We build insulated patios. Here's a quote."
**Grand Slam:** "An extra room for your home that adds $30-50K in property value."

**Value Stack:**
- Custom 3D design & visualisation: $1,500 perceived / $0 actual (Secure Scope)
- Council approval management: $800 / $200
- Premium insulated roofing: $3,000 / $1,200
- LED downlight package: $1,200 / $400
- Full site cleanup: $500 / $150
- 10-year structural warranty: $2,000 / minimal
- Annual inspection for 2 years: $400 / $100/visit
- **Total perceived added value: $9,400+**

**5 Stackable Guarantees:**
1. Price Lock (60 days)
2. Timeline ($500 off if late start, $500 if late finish)
3. Clean Site ($200 off if site dirty at end of day)
4. Quality Inspection (independent third-party included free)
5. Satisfaction (fix before final payment)

## 4. Cross-Sell & Conversion Engine

### Conversion Stats

- **30% close rate is at or above industry average** (20-30% typical for home improvement)
- **Speed-to-lead is critical:** 1-minute response = +391% conversion. After 5 min, odds drop 80%.
- **Tiered pricing:** 4+ options → 52% close rate vs 42% (10pp lift)
- **Video quoting (Loom):** +20% proposal acceptance
- **Finance offering:** +18% close rate, +30% job size

### Cross-Sell Automation

**When:**
1. At scoping: "I noticed your fencing — want me to include?"
2. On the quote: "Recommended additions" section
3. 3 days after patio quote: automated fencing cross-sell email
4. At completion: loyalty pricing for second product
5. 12-month check-in: "Any other outdoor projects?"

**High-margin add-ons:** LED downlights ($500-1,500, 60-70% margin), ceiling fans ($300-800), privacy screens ($1,500-4,000), outdoor blinds ($2,000-5,000)

**Bundle discount:** 5-10% off combined vs individual. Higher margin because of saved mobilisation/admin.

## 7. Customer Experience Design

### The Full Journey

**Stage 1 — First Contact (Speed = Everything)**
- Within 60 seconds: AI phone OR instant SMS acknowledgment
- Webchat qualifies: project type, suburb, size, timeline
- Scoper assigned within 2 hours
- Site visit booked within 24-48 hours

**Stage 2 — Scoping & Quoting**
- Secure Scope for live 3D visualisation
- Mention finance early
- Cross-sell check while on-site
- Tiered quote within 48 hours (Essential/Premium/Signature)
- Loom video walking through the quote
- Present Signature first (anchor high)

**Stage 3 — Follow-Up (Value-Adding)**
- Day 1: Quote + Loom video
- Day 3: Cross-sell email
- Day 5: Social proof (similar project photos)
- Day 7: Finance reminder ("$89/week")
- Day 10: Genuine scarcity (install slots)
- Day 14: Educational content

**Stage 4 — Build & Handover (The Peak-End Moment)**
- Pre-start meeting
- Daily photo updates
- Clean site policy
- Photo documentation at every quality gate
- **Handover:** Clean site → walkthrough → before/after photos → branded handover pack → gift (wine, ~$30-50) → photos together → mention referral program

**Stage 5 — Post-Completion (Referral Engine)**
- Day 1: NPS survey (SMS)
- Promoters (9-10): Google review request + referral invite
- Day 7: Review follow-up
- Day 14: Cross-sell sequence
- Month 6: Check-in call
- Month 12: Anniversary check-in + maintenance plan offer
- Christmas: Card to all past-year customers
