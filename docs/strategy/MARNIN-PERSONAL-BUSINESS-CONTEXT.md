## THE WINTER HEDGE (Emergency Services Strategy)

This is one of the smartest things in the model. Most outdoor living companies have a **seasonal revenue gap** — patio and fencing work drops off in Perth's winter. Insurance/makesafe work doesn't.

**SecureWorks Emergency Services:**
- Makesafes (storm damage, fallen trees, structural emergencies)
- Temporary repairs
- Minor restoration
- Asbestos fencing removal (Category B)
- Emergency call-outs

**Why this matters:**
- B2B revenue from insurance builders and loss adjusters (not weather-dependent)
- Counter-seasonal — busiest when outdoor living is slowest
- Lower skill barrier to entry (can train new hires faster)
- Feeds the talent pipeline (emergency techs become fencing/patio installers)
- Year-round cashflow stability that competitors don't have

**2026 target:** $300K-$500K revenue (Year 1 full commercial push)
**B2B partnerships:** Insurance builders, loss adjusters, body corporates
