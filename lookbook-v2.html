26
YOUR OPTIONS

Choose Your
Material

Composite Decking

The low-maintenance option. Engineered boards that look like timber but never need staining, sealing, or sanding.

  • Won't splinter, warp, or rot
  • Fade-resistant — colours stay true for decades
  • 20+ year lifespan, termite-proof

Best for: Families with kids, pool surrounds, low maintenance.

Timber Decking

Natural warmth and character that no composite can perfectly replicate.

Merbau — Rich reddish-brown. Naturally durable. Our most popular timber. Oil 1-2× per year.

Spotted Gum — Australian hardwood. Stunning grain. Extremely hard and durable.

Treated Pine — Budget-friendly. Requires regular sealing and staining.

Best for: Real wood lovers who don't mind annual maintenance.

We handle the subframe, boards, and finishing. When your deck is part of a complete outdoor living project, the subframe is designed as one integrated system.
