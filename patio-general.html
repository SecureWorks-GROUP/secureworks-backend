The Guide

What Type of Patio is Right for You?

We build them all — so we recommend what's actually best for you. Here's an honest look at every option.

Steel Sheet Patio

Clean, practical Colorbond coverage that matches your existing roof. The reliable choice for carports, alfresco areas, and covered outdoor zones.

Great for: Carports, alfresco shade, side entries
Our take: Perfect for solid coverage without the premium — we can add polycarb lites for natural light too.

Insulated Panel Patio

Roof, insulation, and a flat ceiling in one panel. Up to 22°C cooler, silent in rain, ready for downlights and fans. This is where a patio becomes a room.

Great for: Outdoor dining, entertaining, second living rooms
Our take: If you'll actually live in the space — not just walk through it — insulated is worth every dollar.
Learn more on our Insulated Patios page →

Timber Pergola / Patio

Real timber brings warmth and character that steel can't replicate. Jarrah, merbau, or treated pine — stunning on federation, Hamptons, and coastal homes.

Great for: Character homes, pools, design-led builds
Our take: Pair a timber frame with insulated roofing for the best of both worlds.

Open Pergola / Shade

Posts, beams, and battens — with shade cloth, climbing plants, or left open. The simplest way to define an outdoor space on a budget.

Great for: Garden features, poolside, wisteria structures
Our take: Sometimes simple is exactly right — and we'll tell you when that's the case.
