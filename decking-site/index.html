Good to Know

Council Permits & Builder Protection

We Handle Council Approvals

Decking permits in Perth vary by council — and getting it wrong can mean fines three times the permit cost, or being forced to rip out your brand-new deck. We handle the entire process so you don't deal with council.

  • City of Wanneroo: Permit required for ALL decking
  • City of Joondalup: Permit if >20m², >500mm high, or near boundary
  • Decks over 500mm trigger setback and privacy rules
  • Structural engineering certification included
  • CDC or DA lodgement handled on your behalf

Why a Registered Builder Matters

Most deck builders in Perth are carpenters — not registered builders. For any project over $20,000, WA law requires a registered building contractor. Here's what that means for your protection:

  • Home Indemnity Insurance (mandatory for work over $20K)
  • 6-year statutory defects warranty under WA law
  • Access to the Building Commissioner for disputes
  • Regulated progress payments — no dodgy deposit demands
