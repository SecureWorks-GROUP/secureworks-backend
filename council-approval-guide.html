Your Questions Answered

Do I need to do anything?
Almost nothing. We manage the entire permit process. The only time you may need to help is getting a neighbour consent letter — and we'll walk you through it step by step.
Is the permit fee extra?
No. Council building permit fees (typically $150–300) are already included in your quote. There are no hidden costs or surprise invoices down the line.
What if my property is tricky?
We deal with tight setbacks, sloping blocks, and heritage zones regularly. Our engineer designs around constraints so you get the best outcome without delays.
Can I start before the permit?
No — building before a permit is issued is illegal in WA. But our process is fast, so you won't be waiting long. We keep you updated at every stage.
